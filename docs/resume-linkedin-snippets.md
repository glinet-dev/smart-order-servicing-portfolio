## LinkedIn Post Draft

I built a public Product Owner case study on airline retailing workflows, focusing on Smart Order Servicing and Ancillary Continuity.

The project explores a realistic Offer → Order → Servicing scenario where a customer changes a booking after purchasing paid ancillaries such as seats, baggage, and meals. The focus is not just “change flight,” but the full customer question: what happens to every purchased item before I confirm?

The portfolio includes:

- Product problem and solution framing
- Anonymized competitor-style benchmark
- Epics and 15 user stories
- Acceptance criteria, Definition of Ready, and Definition of Done
- OpenAPI contracts
- NFRs and technical debt backlog
- SAFe PI planning simulation
- Product metrics and target outcomes
